Menu

French Toast

French toast is bread dipped in eggs and milk, then fried until golden. It’s often topped with syrup, sugar, or berries for a sweet breakfast treat.

Croissant

A croissant is a flaky, buttery pastry with a golden, crisp exterior and a soft, layered interior. It’s a classic French treat often enjoyed plain or filled with chocolate, almond paste, or ham and cheese.

Waffles

Waffles are golden, crispy treats with a soft inside, often topped with rich chocolate drizzle, a dollop of whipped cream, and fresh fruit. The toppings settle into the waffle’s pockets, adding a perfect balance of sweetness and flavor to each bite.

French Breakfast

A French breakfast is often light and simple, typically featuring a flaky croissant or baguette with butter and jam, accompanied by a cup of coffee or espresso. Sometimes, fresh fruit or yogurt is added for a balanced, delightful start to the day.

French Breakfast with Jam

A French breakfast often includes a buttery croissant or baguette with sweet jam, paired with coffee or espresso. It’s a simple, satisfying way to start the day with classic flavors.

Ice Coffee

Iced coffee is the perfect pick-me-up for those warm days when you need a burst of energy with a chill vibe! It's a deliciously refreshing brew that starts hot but cools down over ice, turning into a smooth, iced delight. Whether you like it sweet, creamy, or with a splash of flavor, iced coffee is here to keep you refreshed and ready to take on the day with a smile!

Americano

An Americano is a simple yet bold coffee, made by adding hot water to espresso for a smooth, rich flavor. It's the perfect choice when you want a strong coffee taste without the intensity of straight espresso.

Frappuccino

A Frappuccino is a fun, frosty blend of coffee, ice, and creamy goodness. It's sweet, refreshing, and perfect for those moments when you crave a cool, indulgent treat with a caffeine kick!

Chocolate Milkshake

A Chocolate Milkshake is a rich, creamy treat that's like a chocolatey dream in a glass. Blended to perfection with ice cream, milk, and chocolate, it’s the ultimate indulgence when you’re craving something sweet and chilled!

Chocolate Ice Cream

Chocolate ice cream is a decadent, creamy delight that melts in your mouth with every bite. Rich, smooth, and full of chocolatey goodness, it’s the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth!
