Who we are
Founder
Edward
   Meet Edward, the paws-itively passionate founder of Pet & Claws Cafe! With a heart full of love for cats, dogs, and all things cheerful, Edward’s dream was to create a one-of-a-kind space where pets and their owners could come together to enjoy the simple pleasures of life—delicious food, great company, and a whole lot of tail-wagging fun. Inspired by his lifelong love of animals and the joy they bring, Edward envisioned a place where pets aren’t just pets—they’re cherished family members.

His vision was to build a community where furry friends could indulge in tasty treats, sip on dog-friendly milkshakes, and unwind in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere, while their humans could relax, sip coffee, and share special moments together. Every corner of Pet & Claws Cafe was designed to celebrate the bond between pets and people, offering a space for families—both two-legged and four-legged—to connect, create memories, and enjoy life’s little pleasures.		  

Co-Founder
Alice
 Meet Alice, the creative and compassionate co-founder of Pet & Claws Cafe! With a heart as warm as her smile, Alice has always been passionate about creating spaces where animals and humans can share special moments. Her dream was to help bring Edward’s vision to life, adding her unique touch to the cafe’s welcoming atmosphere. Inspired by her love for animals and her background in hospitality, Alice envisioned a place where pets are treated like royalty, and their owners can unwind in comfort.

Her mission is to provide a space where everyone—humans and pets alike—can enjoy quality time, tasty treats, and a relaxed environment. Alice believes that the best memories are made when families—both two-legged and four-legged—are together, and she strives to ensure that every corner of Pet & Claws Cafe feels like home. From pet-friendly snacks to a cozy atmosphere, Alice’s thoughtful touches make the cafe a true haven for all who visit.		  
         
Our story

Pet & Claws Cafe was born from the shared dream of two lifelong animal lovers, Edward and Alice. Both had always envisioned a space where pets and their humans could come together to enjoy delicious food, great company, and create lasting memories. The idea for the cafe sparked in their hometown, where they saw a gap—a place where furry companions and their owners could truly relax and bond in a cozy, welcoming environment.

Edward, with his background in pet care, and Alice, with her expertise in hospitality, combined their talents to bring this vision to life. They dreamt of a cafe that celebrated the unique bond between pets and their families, where pets weren’t just animals, but cherished members of the family. From pet-friendly treats and dog-friendly milkshakes to comfortable seating and cozy corners, every detail of Pet & Claws Cafe was designed with pets in mind.

After months of planning, hard work, and a lot of passion, Pet & Claws Cafe officially opened its doors. From day one, it became a favorite spot for pet lovers to gather and share special moments
with their furry companions. What started as a dream to create a pet-friendly haven has grown into a vibrant community space where both humans and pets can connect, unwind, and enjoy
life’s simple pleasures.

Today, Pet & Claws Cafe continues to thrive as a beloved hub for families—both two-legged and four-legged. Every visit is a chance to create new memories,
celebrate the joy of pets, and experience the warmth of our cafe, all thanks to Edward and Alice’s unwavering dedication to making pets feel at home.
