Events

Paws & Whiskers Wilderness Adventure
Get ready for an unforgettable weekend at Paws & Whiskers Wilderness Adventure, where dogs, cats, and their humans escape the hustle and bustle of daily life to enjoy the great outdoors! Picture this: your furry friends hiking scenic trails, lounging by the campfire, and making new friends while you relax under the stars. We’ve got pet-friendly obstacle courses, thrilling nature walks, and cozy campfire stories to keep everyone entertained. There’ll be special treats, like pet-safe s’mores, and plenty of space for naps and cuddles. Whether your pup loves adventure or your kitty enjoys quiet moments, this camping trip is all about bonding with your furry family while surrounded by nature!

Paws & Whiskers Picnic Day
Pack a blanket, grab your picnic basket, and get ready for a paws-itively amazing day at Paws & Whiskers Picnic Day! This outdoor event is designed for cats, dogs, and their humans to relax, socialize, and enjoy some quality time together. You’ll enjoy delicious snacks (both for you and your furry friends), while your pets play in a safe, pet-friendly space. With games like fetch, tug-of-war, and even interactive play for kitties, there’s something for everyone. Let your pups chase their tails while your kitties enjoy the sunshine—this is the perfect day for laughter, cuddles, and making memories with your four-legged companions!

Paws & Whiskers Adoption Day
Looking for a new furry family member? Don’t miss Paws & Whiskers Adoption Day—the event that brings together the sweetest cats and dogs, all ready to find their forever home! Come meet a diverse group of adorable, adoptable pets and fall in love with your new best friend. There will be playtime, treats, and plenty of opportunities to bond with these lovable pets. Whether you're looking for an energetic pup or a cuddly kitty, you’ll find the perfect match! Don’t miss out on giving a pet a second chance at happiness, and join us for a day filled with love, wagging tails, and purring cuddles!

Paws & Whiskers Play Day
Let the fun begin at Paws & Whiskers Play Day, where dogs and cats come together for the ultimate day of play, laughter, and new friendships! Watch your pets race through obstacle courses, chase after toys, and explore our pet-friendly play zones. There are interactive games for both cats and dogs, so everyone can join the fun! Whether your dog loves a good game of fetch or your cat prefers a quiet corner with some feather toys, we’ve got something for every furry friend. Plus, with tons of treats, water stations, and shady spots to relax, your pets will have the time of their lives. It’s the perfect opportunity for them to stretch their paws, make new pals, and have a blast!
